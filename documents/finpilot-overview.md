# FinPilot

## What FinPilot Is

FinPilot is a mobile app that helps people understand and manage their money in a simple way. It lets users record the money they receive, track what they spend, and ask smart questions before making a purchase.

Instead of only showing numbers and charts, FinPilot acts like a personal money assistant. A user can ask things like:

- Can I afford this right now?
- Am I spending too much this month?
- What do I waste the most money on?
- How can I save better?

FinPilot then looks at the user's income, spending history, and habits to give practical advice.

## The Problem FinPilot Solves

Many people know they should track their money, but most budgeting apps feel too technical, too manual, or too boring to use regularly. People often spend without clearly knowing:

- whether a purchase is financially safe
- where most of their money is going
- what habits are hurting their savings
- how much they should realistically save

As a result, users may overspend, fail to build savings, and make financial decisions without enough clarity.

## How FinPilot Helps

FinPilot solves this problem by combining expense tracking with AI-based guidance.

It helps users:

- log income and expenses in one place
- check whether a planned purchase is reasonable
- understand spending patterns
- find categories where they should cut back
- set savings goals based on real behavior
- view reports and visual summaries of their finances

## Why It Matters

FinPilot is designed for people who do not just want a budget tracker. They want a smarter app that explains their financial situation in normal language and helps them make better decisions.

The goal is to make personal finance feel less confusing, more practical, and more personal.
