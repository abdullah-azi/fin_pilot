Design decisions

Net hero card leads

The month's net saved is the headline number — bigger than income or expenses alone. The split income/expense bar below it shows the ratio visually before the user reads a single figure.

Savings rate ring

60.2% saved this month with a circular progress ring — the single most motivating number in the report. Comparing to last month (54%) gives it context without clutter.

Grouped bar chart — current highlighted

Past months are faded; May is full-brightness; June is dashed outline. The user always knows where they are in time without a legend or axis label.

Category table with VS LAST column

The third column (↑↓→ vs last month) makes the table scannable in seconds. Red/green arrows are more readable than percentage numbers alone.

Largest transactions

Reports should answer "where did the big money go?" The top 3 transactions answer that without requiring the user to scroll through all 47 entries.

4 export options

PDF for sharing with a financial advisor, CSV for personal spreadsheets, WhatsApp for sharing with family, Email for records. All four are common real-world needs in the target market.

Ask AI shortcut

Same pattern as spending analysis — after reading the report, the natural next question is "so what should I do differently?" The violet card bridges straight to the assistant.
